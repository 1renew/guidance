Environmental Land Management

Prototyped guidance

Guidance for 18 March 2020

Taken from the ELM action list provided by JLP

Use cover crops to maintain carbon storage and reduce sediment in water bodies

Increase organic matter in soils to reduce wind erosion

Provide drinking water for livestock to reduce the amount of sediment in water bodies

Use lower emission fertilisers to reduce ammonia and greenhouse gas emissions

Fence off water sources from livestock to prevent erosion and contamination

Create or maintain hill slope tree shelter belts to reduce flooding
